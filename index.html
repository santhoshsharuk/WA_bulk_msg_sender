Why Choose This Tool?

Our script is built with simplicity and power in mind, offering features that make your messaging campaigns effective and easy to manage.

Bulk Sending

Effortlessly send messages to hundreds of contacts from a single CSV file.

Personalization

Use {name} placeholders to create unique, engaging messages for each contact.

Session Persistence

Scan the QR code once. The script securely saves your session for future use.
